# SnowCamp

> The conference driving you (wal)nuts

## What is SnowCamp?

<div>
<Polaroid title="universities, conferences, unconference" img="/img/snowcamp-equation.webp" side="right" />

First and foremost, it's a highly technical conference where IT professionals from around the world come to share their knowledge of today's and tomorrow's technologies.
But SnowCamp is also a team of volunteer geeks driven by the idea of organizing the one and only conference of its kind in Grenoble!
Finally, SnowCamp is a concept of its own: conferences and workshops on Thursday and Friday; and on Saturday, a relaxing day in the snow.

</div>

# Grenoble and the innovation

<div>
<Polaroid title="Grenoble by night" img="/img/grenoble.webp" side="left" />

Since the dawn of electricity, Grenoble has always been a hub of innovation and excellence: home to cutting-edge labs and industries, labeled "French Tech" for over 10 years, and now crowned [European Capital of Innovation 2026](https://www.grenoblealpesmetropole.fr/1136-grenoble-alpes-metropole-capitale-de-l-innovation-2026.htm).
Here, technical innovation has taken root in the surrounding mountains — and that's what sets us apart.
</div>

# Conferences

<div>
<Polaroid title="A talk" img="/img/ozan.webp" side="right" />
SnowCamp is built around three main events:

- the Conference and workshops, on Thursday and Friday: 20 minutes to 2 hours presentations. In 2017, the conference was structured around several tracks,
- the Meet & Greet, on Thursday evening: a gathering that gives the opportunity to meet and share with other attendees
- the Unconference, on Saturday, offers to speakers and attendees an opportunity to meet each other on the slopes of the Grenoble area.

The first edition back in 2016 gathered ~200 attendees and speakers in Grenoble university building, and grew with your support to be hosted in Grenoble's World Trade Center to gather ~600 attendees and ~70 speakers since 2020.
Since 2026, SnowCamp has been held at Espace 1968 in Alpexpo, welcoming over 650 attendees.
</div>

# The unconference

<div>
<Polaroid title="The unconference" img="/img/unconference.jpg" side="left" />

A conference is more than just attending presentations, it's also the discussions with the other participants, meeting the speakers...
What better to continue these exchanges other than a day in the open air? SnowCamp ends with a day on the skiing slopes, in order to take advantage of the exceptional surroundings of Grenoble.
