## Le SnowCamp evolve for 2027

For its 11th edition, SnowCamp is evolving and switching to a two-day format, still followed by the mountain unconference on Saturday for presenters.

SnowCamp 2027 will take place on Thursday, January 14, and Friday, January 15, at Alpexpo, Espace 1968.

The workshops will continue but will be held in a 2-hour format and run concurrently with other sessions over the two days. Space in these workshops will be limited to 20 participants, on a first-come, first-served basis.

By shortening the duration and scheduling them on Thursday and Friday, we hope to encourage proposals during the Call for Papers and facilitate participation in the workshops.

These changes are the result of careful consideration regarding how to streamline the event for the organizing team and keep prices low so that everyone can participate while maintaining a balanced budget.

We look forward to seeing you on January 14 and 15, 2027.

The SnowCamp Team.
