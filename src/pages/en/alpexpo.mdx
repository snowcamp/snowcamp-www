## Come to SnowCamp

### Public transport

The easiest way is by public transport. All information is on [reso-m.fr](https://www.reso-m.fr/). Key points:
- You need a ticket, which you must validate:
    - At a machine outside tram stops, or directly on the bus
    - Again at each change of tram or bus. Ticket is valid for 1 hour
- Tickets can be bought:
    - At machines at tram stops
    - By [credit card](https://www.reso-m.fr/696-achetez-et-validez-par-carte-bancaire-.htm): tap your card on the validator – price €2
    - By [SMS](https://www.reso-m.fr/249-les-tickets-par-sms.htm): send "1H" to 93123 for a 1-hour ticket. Price €2.50
    - Via the [M app](https://www.reso-m.fr/674-l-appli-m-pour-me-deplacer-au-quotidien.htm#par13357)

More details and prices: [Tarifs & Vente](https://www.reso-m.fr/67-un-tarif-pour-chacun.htm).
The [M app](https://www.reso-m.fr/674-l-appli-m-pour-me-deplacer-au-quotidien.htm#par13357) offers schedules, itineraries, and ticket purchase.

If you arrive at Grenoble station and go directly to Alpexpo:
- Take tram A from near the station
- Departure: Grenoble train station, direction Pont-de-Claix L’étoile
- Arrival: Polesud – Alpexpo, then walk (follow posted signs)
- Time: ~30 minutes

![Route from the station](/img/speakers_guide/trajet_gare.png)

From the city centre to Alpexpo:
- Take tram A from Victor Hugo square
- Departure: Victor Hugo
- Arrival: Polesud – Alpexpo, then walk (follow posted signs)
- Time: ~25 minutes

![Route from the city centre](/img/speakers_guide/trajet_centre_ville.png)

If arriving by plane, there are shuttle buses:
- From Lyon Saint-Exupéry ([BlaBlaBus](https://www.blablacar.fr/search?fn=Saint-Exup%C3%A9ry%2C+V%C3%A9nissieux%2C+France&tn=Grenoble%2C+Place+de+la+Gare%2C+Grenoble%2C+France&db=2026-01-14&seats=1&search_origin=SEARCH&transport_type=BUS&from_place_id=eyJpIjoiQ2hJSm80TTRVSGpDOUVjUndjaWYyQ3ZDcy1VIiwicCI6MSwidiI6MSwidCI6W119&to_place_id=eyJpIjoiQ2hJSmE0b2Nhb24waWtjUmIyM19ENmxzdGdZIiwicCI6MSwidiI6MSwidCI6WzJdfQ%3D%3D&p0%5Bac%5D=adult) or [FlixBus](https://shop.flixbus.fr/search?departureCity=09e56f73-34a7-4b38-bf5f-49443dc4000c&arrivalCity=40dfef30-8646-11e6-9066-549f350fcb0c&route=A%C3%A9roport+de+Lyon+Saint-Exup%C3%A9ry-Grenoble&rideDate=14.01.2026&adult=1&_locale=fr&departureCountryCode=FR&arrivalCountryCode=FR))
- From Geneva Cointrin (3 trips/day, 7 days/week) to Grenoble bus station, then public transport to the venue.

### Car
